# 🚀 SaaS Directories: 100+ Places to Promote Your SaaS

Discover the best SaaS directories, software review sites, startup communities, and product launch platforms to grow your SaaS.

Whether you're launching a new product or scaling an established SaaS, listing on platforms like G2, Capterra, Product Hunt, GetApp, AlternativeTo, and Trustpilot can help you improve SEO, earn high-quality backlinks, increase brand visibility, generate qualified leads, collect customer reviews, and acquire more customers.

## 🎯 Why Submit Your SaaS?

Listing your SaaS on relevant directories can help you:

* Improve SEO with authoritative backlinks
* Increase domain authority
* Generate targeted referral traffic
* Build trust through verified reviews
* Improve product discoverability
* Increase brand awareness
* Attract qualified leads
* Boost signups and revenue growth
